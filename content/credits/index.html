Where the photos come from.

WorldDishAtlas is in the brand-placeholder phase for hero photography. Every dish currently carries a programmatically-generated card (gradient + dish name) rather than a stock photo. They look intentional, not broken.

The plan is to replace these with original food photography over time, commissioned dish by dish. When that happens, credits will move to a per-image attribution table on this page.

  • Per-dish hero cards at /img/dish-<slug>.jpg: generated in-house. Gradient + dish name in serif. WorldDishAtlas wordmark in the corner.
