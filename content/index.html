How this site works

Every dish on WorldDishAtlas is researched, written, and edited by Lewis Vaughan, with help from a pipeline that combines AI-assisted research and a human-verified provenance record on every entry. The verified date you see at the bottom of every dish page is the day the editor last cross-checked that record against its primary source.

The full pipeline is on the methodology page. See about WorldDishAtlas for context, or our sister site TableJourney for where to eat the dishes in their canonical cities.
